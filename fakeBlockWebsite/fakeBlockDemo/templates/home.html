No more fake news.

FakeBlock is a Chrome browser extension to prevent you from becoming misinformed by your Facebook news feed.
Utilizing a mixture of AI and the Google fact checking, FakeBlock meticulously examines each post to determine its legitimacy before allowing you to see it.

Give it a whack!

DEMO

Just type something into the text box and click Post, as if it were Facebook! FakeBlock will analyze your "post" and behave like the Chrome extension does so that you can get a taste for what FakeBlock can do for you.
